---
title: "Sharing with team"
description: "Share a prompt with your team."
---

To enhance the collaboration between your team, you can share a prompt with your team. This will help you to collaborate with your team and improve the quality of your LLM outputs.

Go to the Editor panel on the [Prompts page](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/prompts) and click the `Link` button.
