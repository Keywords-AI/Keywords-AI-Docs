---
title: "Custom metadata"
---

You can add any key-value pair to this metadata field for your reference, contact team@keywordsai.co if you need extra parameter support for your use case.

You will see your metadata in the right side bar on the Logs page in the Keywords AI platform.

<img width="300" src="/images/api-features/metadata.jpg"/>

## Example:

```json
{
  "node_id": "qaisrgPyKr4NSugZeOqRt"
}
```
