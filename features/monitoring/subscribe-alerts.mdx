Keywords AI's Alerts and Warnings system enables real-time monitoring of your application's status. Receive instant email notifications for LLM outages and monitor warning logs through our dashboard.

## Subscribe to alerts
To manage your alert preferences, navigate to [Alerts](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/alerts-and-warnings). You can toggle notifications and select specific alerts you wish to receive.

## Warnings
[Warnings](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/alerts-and-warnings/warnings) shows all configured warnings that appear in Logs. You can customize which warnings are displayed.
