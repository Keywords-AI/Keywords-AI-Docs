Keywords AI's Alerts and Warnings system helps you monitor your application's status in real-time. Get instant email notifications for LLM outages and track warning logs through our dashboard.

## Subscribe to alerts
Visit the [Alerts page](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/alerts-and-warnings) to manage your alert preferences. Toggle the subscription button and select which alerts you'd like to receive.

## Warnings

The [Warnings page](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/alerts-and-warnings/warnings) displays all configured warnings that will appear in your Logs page. You can customize the warnings to display on the Logs page.

</Frame>

Once warnings are configured, they will be visible in your Logs page.
