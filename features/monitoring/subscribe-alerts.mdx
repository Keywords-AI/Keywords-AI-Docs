title: Alerts & Warnings
description: Subscribe to system status and get notified via email when an LLM outage is detected. 
---

On Keywords AI, you can build a reliable AI app by using our reliability features. Alerts and Warnings are two features that help you monitor your app's status. You will get notified via email when an LLM outage is detected, or easily find the warning logs in the Logs page.

## Subscribe to alerts
Go to the [Alerts page](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/alerts-and-warnings), you can click the Toggle button to subscribe to alerts, and then choose the alerts you want to receive.
<Frame>
<img src="https://keywordsai-static.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/observability/alerts.png"/>
</Frame>

## Warnings
You can go to the [Warnings page](https://platform.keywordsai.co/platform/alerts-and-warnings/warnings) to see all the warnings you want to display on the Logs page. This provides better visibility into your LLM usage.

<img src="https://keywordsai-static.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/observability/warnings.png"/>
</Frame>

After you set up warnings, you can see the warnings on the Logs page.
