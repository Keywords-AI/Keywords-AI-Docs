---
title: "Views"
description: "A guide to create filtered views of traces that you can save and share with others in your organization."
---

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a filter">
You should first create a filter to filter traces.
<Frame>
<img src="https://keywordsai-static.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/traces/views/filters.jpg" alt="Trace Filters" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Add more filters">
You can add more filters to the view by clicking the **Add filter** button.
<Frame>
<img src="https://keywordsai-static.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/traces/views/add-a-filter.jpg" alt="Add a filter" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Save a view">
Then, click the **Save** button to save the view and you can name it as you want.
<Frame>
<img src="https://keywordsai-static.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/traces/views/save-a-view.jpg" alt="Save" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Select the view">
After saving the view, you can select the view from the dropdown menu.
<Frame>
<img src="https://keywordsai-static.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/docs/traces/views/select-a-view.jpg" alt="Select a view" />
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>
